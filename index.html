The Legacy of BMW M

1972

Birth of BMW M

BMW Motorsport GmbH was established, marking the beginning of the legendary M division.

1978

First M Car

The iconic BMW M1 was introduced, the first car developed solely by BMW M.

1984

M5 Debut

The first BMW M5 was launched, combining luxury with high performance.
